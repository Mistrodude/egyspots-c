Privacy Policy

EgySpots | Last updated: April 2026

1. What We Collect

  • Account info: email address, display name, username, phone number, date of birth, gender, city
  • Location data: GPS coordinates when you use check-in or locate features (only while using the app)
  • User-generated content: photos you upload as stories, spot descriptions you write
  • Usage data: check-ins, story views, app interactions (via Firebase Analytics)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To show you nearby hangout spots on the map
  • To enable check-ins, stories, and community features
  • To send in-app notifications about spot activity
  • To enforce our age gate (13+) and Terms of Service

3. Data Retention

  • Stories are automatically deleted after 6 hours via Firestore TTL policy
  • Account data is retained until you delete your account
  • Check-in history is deleted when you delete your account

4. Third Parties

  • Firebase (Google) — database, authentication, storage, analytics
  • OpenStreetMap / CARTO — map tiles (no personal data shared)
  • Apple / Google — if you use Sign in with Apple or Google

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. To delete your account and all associated data, use Settings → Delete Account inside the app, or email us at privacy@egyspots.com.

6. Children

EgySpots is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Egypt Law 151 of 2020

EgySpots processes personal data in compliance with Egyptian Law No. 151 of 2020 on the Protection of Personal Data. Data is processed on a lawful basis, limited to stated purposes, and protected by appropriate technical measures.
