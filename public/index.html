About

Built in Cairo
for Cairo

We got tired of finding out about the best spots in the city after they were over — through Instagram DMs and WhatsApp groups. So we built a live map where you can see where people are actually hanging out right now.

No restaurants, no reviews, no algorithms. Just real spots, real people, updated every six hours.

"The best nights in Cairo have always spread by word of mouth. We just made the word travel faster."
